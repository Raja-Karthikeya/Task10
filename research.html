What are the specifications of BMW M1000RR?

The BMW M 1000 RR is powered by a 999 CC engine. This M 1000 RR engine generates a power of 156 kW (212hp) at 14,500 rpm with a max torque of 113 NM at 11,000 rpm. The BMW M 1000 RR is powered by a Water/oil-cooled 4-cylinder with 4-stroke in-line engine, four titanium valves per cylinder and BMW ShiftCam.
